Privacy Butler is part of research project conducted at the University of Oxford. As part of this project, your anonymized data will be collected and may be used to analyse clusters of websites that generate privacy concerns. The data sent back for analysis will include (1) your anonymous user id, (2). the URLs of the websites on which Privacy Butler was used, and (3) the level of concern for those websites. Your data will be added to a group analysis, to identify websites generating concern, and it will NOT be used to personally identify you in any way. Your continued use of the extension indicates consent to be apart of the research project. If you would like to opt-out, please discontinue using this application. Once you begin using this application, your anonymous data will be collected, we will not be able to identify and delete that data should you wish to withdraw. However, ALL data will be deleted at the completion of this project. For any questions or comments, contact the researcher at olga.musayev@oii.ox.ac.uk, AND/OR the Oxford Ethics Committee at ethics@oii.ox.ac.uk.

Privacy Butler uses Electronic Frontier Foundation's Privacy Badger to power its front-end, which is built on top of Adblock Plus. Behold the power of open-source.
